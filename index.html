💻NEFC AI Association Program Launched at NIELIT

NIELIT Updates | Campus News

The NIELIT campus recently witnessed the launch of the NEFC AI Association Program encouraging students to explore Artificial Intelligence.

Read Full Article

The program focuses on practical learning, collaboration, and innovation among students interested in AI development. The association plans to organize workshops, coding sessions, guest lectures, and project-based learning activities throughout the academic session. Faculty members emphasized that AI skills are becoming essential across industries and encouraged students from all departments to participate. Students expressed excitement about the initiative, stating that the program provides an opportunity to gain real-world exposure beyond classroom learning. The organizers also hinted at upcoming hackathons and collaborative AI projects. The NEFC AI Association is expected to become a major platform for technical growth within the campus community.

🏆Annual Student Sports Event Begins on the 15th

NIELIT Updates | Sports Desk

The much-anticipated Student Sports Event officially began on the 15th of this month and will continue until the 25th, bringing energy and enthusiasm across the NIELIT campus.

Read Full Article

Various indoor and outdoor competitions have been scheduled, including football, badminton, cricket, volleyball, and athletics events. Students from multiple departments are actively participating, promoting teamwork and healthy competition. According to organizers, the primary goal of the event is to encourage physical fitness and strengthen student interaction outside academic activities. Spectators have gathered in large numbers to support their classmates, creating a lively atmosphere throughout the campus. Winners of each category will be awarded certificates and prizes during the closing ceremony scheduled at the end of the event.
